Welcome To Bellwether Investment

Bellwether Investment is a web based portal which familiarizes users with stock market investments/trading with help of machine learning algorithms. The users can buy and sell stocks virtually and gain profits. The users are provided with buying and selling price hints, i.e. at what price they should buy or sell a particular stock. Also they are advised on what stock to but in order maximize their profits. There are various algorithms from which the user can select the future course of action of trading stocks.

The algorithms predict the future prices of the stock on daily, weekly and monthly basis. The users can also perform analysis from various graphs of the companies on different parameters. They will also be able to see the historic/past data of the company and generate graph of their choice of parameters. All the basic functionalities like live feeding of the stock market, order details, portfolio/profile, virtual buy and sell, signup/sign in etc. is also provided.
